[justify]My name is Samuel Lerbet. I am a fourth-year *élève* of the [Département de mathématiques et applications](https://www.math.ens.psl.eu/) at the [École normale supérieure](https://www.ens.psl.eu/) in Paris, and I will start a PhD in September 2022 at [Université Grenoble Alpes](https://www.univ-grenoble-alpes.fr/) under the supervision of [Jean Fasel](https://www-fourier.univ-grenoble-alpes.fr/~faselj/index.html). I am interested in algebraic geometry and specifically in the homotopy theory of schemes and related themes: invariants like Morel's motivic Brouwer degree, Chow groups and Chow–Witt groups... The provisional title of my PhD thesis is: “Un analogue réel de la conjecture de Hodge” (in English: “A real analogue of the Hodge conjecture”).[/justify]
