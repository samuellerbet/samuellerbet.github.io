<p style="text-align:justify;">My name is Samuel Lerbet. I am a fourth-year <em>élève</em> of the <a href="https://www.math.ens.psl.eu/">Département de mathématiques et applications</a> at the <a href="https://www.ens.psl.eu/">École normale supérieure</a> in Paris, and I will start a PhD in September 2022 at <a href="https://www.univ-grenoble-alpes.fr/">Université Grenoble Alpes</a> under the supervision of <a href="https://www-fourier.univ-grenoble-alpes.fr/~faselj/index.html">Jean Fasel</a>. I am interested in algebraic geometry and specifically in the homotopy theory of schemes and related themes: invariants like Morel's motivic Brouwer degree, Chow groups and Chow–Witt groups... The provisional title of my PhD thesis is: “Un analogue réel de la conjecture de Hodge” (in English: “A real analogue of the Hodge conjecture”).</p>
